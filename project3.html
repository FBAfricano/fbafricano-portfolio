About this project

A personal spiritual growth tool that delivers a daily verse and lets users reflect through a Notion-style journaling system. Users can write, tag, and organize entries, link reflections to scripture, and track their devotional habits in a clean, intuitive interface.

Features & Functions

  • ◆ Dashboard: Displays the Verse of the Day fetched from an external API, with reference and optional devotional thought. Quick access buttons allow users to write journal entries, view past entries, search verses, and track streaks.
  • ◆ Journal: Offers a Notion-like rich-text editor for entries, supporting bold, italic, lists, headings, and optional embeds (images, audio, video). Entries can be tagged (e.g., “gratitude”, “prayer request”), viewed in calendar or timeline format, and linked to the day’s verse. Auto date/time stamping and search by keyword, tag, or date are included.
  • ◆ Notifications: Daily reminders to read the verse or write in the journal, supporting habit formation through streaks or badges.
